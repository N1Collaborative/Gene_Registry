Status Terminology

Clinical Application: The therapy has been fully developed and has been administered to at least one patient globally.
Under Development: The therapy is currently under development. This can be at different stages of the development, e.g. very early phases during which compounds are screening pre-clinically, animal testing, or at manufacturing stage. For more information on the progress of one of the projects under development, please reach out to the respective contact person.
